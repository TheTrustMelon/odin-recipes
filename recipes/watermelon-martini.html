Trust's Watermelon Martini

A delectable Summer treat or an alcoholic's melony dream (assuming the alcoholic likes watermelon). This martini is sure to dazzle any and all guests that are invited to that sad
excuse for a get-together you call a "party." It's okay, not all of us can have a nice below ground pool like Todd. That's why you didn't invite Todd. Don't be like Todd. You don't need
Todd, you have the two friends you invited to this "party" and a delicious drink that only you should know the ingredients to!

The glass, chair, TV, remote, and/or straw can count as your two friends if you're really that desperate, although you should really think about making some friends and drinking
less.


Prep Time

10 minutes

Total Time

10 minutes, unless you count drinking time, then 11 minutes

Servings

2, but knowing you, you're drinking both alone



Ingredients

  • Salt as needed, although your liver is probably already a lost cause, so use as much as you want
  • White Sugar as needed
  • 1 cup Watermelon Juice
  • 1/4 Cup Vodka, although your liver is probably already a lost cause, so use as much as you want
  • 1/4 Cup Watermelon Schnapps, although your liv- I'm sure you know the rest
  • 2 Tablespoons Simple Syrup
  • 1 Lime, juiced

    Directions

    1. Combine salt and sugar in a small bowl and pour onto a small, shallow plate. Moisten the rims of 2 glasses with a piece of watermelon and dip the moistened glass rims into the
      salt and sugar mixture; set aside.
    2. Pour watermelon juice, vodka, watermelon schnapps, simple syrup, and lime juice into a cocktail shaker over ice. Cover and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted.
      Strain into the prepared glasses.

      Tips

      To make watermelon juice, process chunks of seedless watermelon in your blender and then strain through cheesecloth. Good luck going into a Walmart and going up to an associate
      going "Hello there, where can I find your cheesecloths?" They're going to look at you like you're either pretentious or just plain weird. My money is on the latter.
